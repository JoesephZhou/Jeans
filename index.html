History

Prairie Lights

Before Red River College Polytechnic's Hospitality, Baking and Culinary Arts students moved downtown into Jane's and the Paterson Global Foods Institute, they operated a restaurant called Prairie Lights at the Notre Dame Campus.

Jane's

The jewel in the GlobalFoods Institute's crown

Jane's treats diners to a new experience in urban upscale, while providing hands-on training opportunities for students in Hospitality and Culinary Arts. The restaurant occupies the site of the building's former bank hall, an ornate and awe-inspiring space that serves as a stunning architectural centrepiece.

Jane's provides a training ground for Red River College Polytechnic's Hospitality and Culinary Arts students to hone their skills. From using state-of-the-art equipment in the kitchen to catering to their customers' needs, they get the hands-on experience needed to succeed in the industry.
